Top 10 places to visit in Idukki


Idukki is a land of serenity, peace, and natural beauty. This place is quickly becoming a popular tourist destination. A holiday in Idukki should be an excellent experience. Several tourist places in Idukki will make you want to stay forever. You will never get tired of the spectacular landscapes and beautiful weather that Idukki offers, especially if you like spending time outdoors. From stunning waterfalls to picturesque mountains, there is something for everyone in this breathtaking region of Kerala.
