Trivandrum or Thiruvananthapuram is the capital of Kerala State in the south of India. It is known for its calm beaches, backwaters, hill stations, and rich culture because of which it attracts several Indian as well as foreign tourists.

Kottayam

Kottayam with a vast network of canals and waterways hosts so many boat races. Festive seasons are the time of boat races.Vagamon and Elaveezhapoonchira are the beautiful hill stations in Kottayam.Kottayam is blessed with nature’s bounty in abundance.

Pathanamthitta

The Pathanamthitta district has much to offer to the visitor, including the beautiful rolling hills and mountains of the Western Ghats and the Periyar river and its tributaries such as the Achankovil river, the Manimala river, the Meenachil river, etc

Ernakulam

Ernakulam is blessed with many serene backwaters. The backwaters separating Ernakulam from Fort Kochi and Mattancherry dotted with many small and big islands is a wonderful sight to see. A boat ride through this backwaters will be an amazing experience. This is the best way to explore the beauty of colourful nature.
